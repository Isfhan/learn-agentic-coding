# 📚 Books, Essays & Papers

## 🏆 The canon (read these first)

These ~10 pieces are referenced again and again across this roadmap.

1. 📄 **[Anthropic — Building effective agents](https://www.anthropic.com/research/building-effective-agents)** — the single best essay on agent design.
2. 📄 **[Anthropic — Writing tools for agents](https://www.anthropic.com/engineering/writing-tools-for-agents)** — required for MCP/tool authors.
3. 📄 **[Anthropic — Multi-agent research system](https://www.anthropic.com/research/multi-agent-research-system)** — how orchestrator-worker patterns play out in practice.
4. 📄 **[Simon Willison — "Tools in a loop" (agents defined)](https://simonwillison.net/2025/May/22/tools-in-a-loop/)** — clearest 1-paragraph definition of an agent.
5. 📄 **[Lance Martin — Context engineering](https://rlancemartin.github.io/2025/06/23/context_engineering/)** — foundational essay on the skill of 2026.
6. 📄 **[ReAct: Synergizing Reasoning and Acting (Yao et al., 2022)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.03629)** — the paper every modern agent descends from.
7. 📄 **[Lost in the middle (Liu et al., 2023)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.03172)** — why bigger context windows aren't the answer.
8. 📄 **[OWASP Top 10 for LLM Applications](https://owasp.org/www-project-top-10-for-large-language-model-applications/)** — security canon.
9. 📄 **[Hamel Husain — "Your AI product needs evals"](https://hamel.dev/blog/posts/evals/)** — eval gospel.
10. 📄 **[Simon Willison — Prompt injection tag feed](https://simonwillison.net/tags/prompt-injection/)** — years of invaluable commentary.
